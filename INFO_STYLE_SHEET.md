## Style Rules

### Punctuation
- **Em-dash (—):** No spaces. Use for parentheticals.
- **Ellipsis (…):** Single character, not three periods.
- **Quotes:** Curly/smart quotes. Periods and commas inside.

### Italics
- Retained Korean terms (first occurrence)
- Book/film/artwork titles
- Internal thoughts
- Date stamps: *Morning, July 28, 2025*

### Chapter Structure
- Date stamp in italics (when present)
- End with: *To be continued*
- No chapter titles—numbered only

### Voice
- Literary but unaffected
- Contemplative, not slow
- Warm, not clinical
- Varied sentence length
- Avoid translationese

### Dialogue
- Natural, unhurried
- Age-appropriate (mature adults)
- Preserve meaningful Korean speech patterns
