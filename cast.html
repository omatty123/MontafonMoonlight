Cast of Characters

Main Characters

Jeong Chanju

정찬주 • Dharma name: Muyeom (무염)

The protagonist - a Buddhist novelist and author who travels to Austria

Park Myeongsuk

박명숙 • Dharma name: Muryanggwang (무량광)

Mrs. Park - ceramic artist and Jeong Chanju's wife

Herbert Willi

헤르베르트 빌리

Austrian composer from Montafon, friend of the Jeongs

Werner Stockinger

베르너 슈토킹거 • Also known as: Dr. Werner, "Dr. Bae," Bae Hoon-a (배훈아)

Austrian friend and guide, known for his love of Korean culture

Kwon Suk-nyeo

권숙녀 • Also known as: Sonja Steindl-Kwon

Chairwoman Kwon - Korean expatriate in Austria

Historical & Religious Figures

Venerable Beopjeong

법정

Renowned Korean Zen master, author of Musoyu (Non-possession), lived at Bulil-am hermitage

Venerable Seongcheol

성철

Famous Korean Zen master

Syngman Rhee

이승만

Dr. Rhee - Korea's first president, historical figure

Francesca Donner

프란체스카 도너

Mrs. Francesca - wife of Dr. Rhee

Hong Gisam

홍기삼

Professor Hong - former president of Dongguk University

King Sejong

세종대왕

King Sejong the Great - creator of Hangeul

Shinmi

신미

Buddhist monk who helped King Sejong create Hangeul

Admiral Yi Sun-sin

이순신

Korean naval commander, hero of the Imjin War, inventor of the turtle ship (geobukseon)
